### [1] [VideoKR: Towards Knowledge- and Reasoning-Intensive Video Understanding](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05259)
*Lin Fu,Zheyuan Yang,Yang Wang,Tingyu Song,Arman Cohan,Yilun Zhao*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 提出了VideoKR，首个大规模知识密集型视频推理训练语料库，包含31.5万视频推理样例和14.5万新收集的专家领域视频，通过人机协作的技能导向生成流程构建，并建立了专家标注的评估基准VideoKR-Eval。实验表明，在SFT→GRPO流程下，基于VideoKR训练的模型在知识密集型视频推理上优于此前方法，同时保持通用视频推理的竞争力。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有视频理解模型在需要深度知识和推理能力的任务上表现不足，缺乏专门针对知识密集型视频推理的大规模训练数据。

Method: 构建了包含31.5万视频推理样例和14.5万CC许可专家领域视频的大规模语料库；采用人机协作、面向技能的样例生成流程，确保难度、多样性和可靠性；同时构建了专家标注的评估基准VideoKR-Eval。

Result: 在SFT→GRPO流程下，基于VideoKR训练的模型在知识密集型视频推理任务上优于此前方法，在通用视频推理上也保持竞争力。

Conclusion: 数据设计是推动视频推理能力进步的关键驱动力，VideoKR为知识密集型视频理解提供了有效支撑。

Abstract: We introduce VideoKR, the first large-scale training corpus specifically designed to strengthen knowledge- and reasoning-intensive video understanding. It comprises 315K video reasoning examples over 145K newly collected, CC-licensed, expert-domain videos. We develop a human-in-the-loop, skill-oriented example generation pipeline that targets progressively deeper video reasoning capabilities while ensuring the difficulty, diversity, and reliability of both the examples and their CoT rationales. We also curate VideoKR-Eval, a new expert-annotated benchmark where questions require genuine video understanding and knowledge-intensive reasoning rather than textual shortcuts. Our experiments show that, under a standard SFT$\rightarrow$GRPO pipeline, models post-trained on VideoKR outperform prior post-training approaches on knowledge-intensive video reasoning while remaining competitive on general video reasoning, highlighting data design as a key driver of progress in video reasoning. We further conduct comprehensive ablations to isolate the contributions of VideoKR, providing actionable insights for future work.

### [2] [Multi-Scale Markov Switching GARCH](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06190)
*Jayesh Chaudhary*

Main category: q-fin.ST

TL;DR: 本文提出三重时间框架的马尔可夫转换GARCH模型，分别对欧元/美元的日线、4小时和1小时波动率进行独立建模，并通过外积构造27状态跨尺度概率张量，实现更优的波动率预测。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 金融波动率存在显著的非平稳性，单一体制模型不足以刻画不断变化的市场状态，因此需要多尺度、多体制的建模框架。

Method: 分别对日线、4小时和1小时三个时间框架估计独立的AR(1)-MS-GARCH模型，在短时间框架中引入Filardo时变转移概率（TVTP），并通过外积构造27状态跨尺度概率张量。

Result: 模型能够识别出统计上显著区分的Calm（平静）、Turbulent（波动）、Crisis（危机）三种体制，且相比传统GARCH基准模型，在样本外波动率预测上表现更优。

Conclusion: 波动率动态在不同时间尺度上存在有意义的结构，分别建模各时间尺度比单一尺度方法能更充分地刻画市场状态。

Abstract: Financial volatility exhibits substantial non-stationarity, making single-regime models inadequate for characterising changing market conditions. This paper proposes a triple-timeframe Markov-Switching GARCH (MS-GARCH) framework for volatility regime detection in EUR/USD across daily, four-hour, and hourly horizons. Three independent AR(1)-MS-GARCH models are estimated to capture macro, meso, and micro regime dynamics, while Filardo-style time-varying transition probabilities (TVTP) are incorporated at the shorter horizons through composite stress indicators. The resulting regime probabilities are combined through an outer-product construction into a 27-state cross-scale probability tensor. Using EUR/USD data from 2015-2025, the framework produces statistically distinct Calm, Turbulent, and Crisis regimes and achieves superior out-of-sample volatility forecasting performance relative to a conventional GARCH benchmark. The results suggest that volatility dynamics contain meaningful structure across multiple timescales and that modelling these scales separately provides a more informative representation of market conditions than a single-timescale approach.

### [3] [FORTE: FOL-guided Optimal Refinement for Text-audio rEtrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05812)
*Arghya Pal,Sailaja Rajanala*

Main category: cs.MM

TL;DR: FORTE通过一阶逻辑推理与参数高效跨模态对齐，显著提升了文本到音频检索的细粒度语义匹配精度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有CLAP、Pengi等共享嵌入模型在文本与音频之间存在固有的模态差距，难以实现细粒度的语义对齐，导致检索精度受限。

Method: 1) 将查询转化为一阶逻辑表示；2) 通过约束搜索在保持语义不变的同时引入判别性属性；3) 使用轻量投影模块与音频嵌入对齐；4) 在推理时通过谓词感知的重排序强制逻辑一致性。

Result: 在AudioCaps和Clotho数据集上，相比强基线方法取得了持续改进，尤其在细粒度场景下表现突出。

Conclusion: 符号推理与表示学习相结合的方法对于跨模态检索是有效的。

Abstract: Text-to-audio retrieval has made significant progress with shared embedding models such as CLAP and Pengi, yet they often struggle with fine-grained semantic alignment due to the inherent modality gap between text and audio. In this work, we propose FORTE, a unified framework that integrates structured logical reasoning with parameter-efficient cross-modal alignment to improve retrieval precision. Our approach first transforms queries into first-order logic and refines them via a constrained search that preserves semantic invariance while introducing discriminative attributes. The refined representation is then aligned with audio embeddings using a lightweight projection module, followed by a predicate-aware re-ranking step that enforces logical consistency at inference. Extensive experiments on AudioCaps and Clotho demonstrate consistent improvements over strong baselines, particularly in challenging fine-grained scenarios. Our results highlight the effectiveness of combining symbolic reasoning with representation learning for cross-modal retrieval.

### [4] [Anti-Hyperspectral Anomaly Detection: A First Study on Stealthy Lipschitz-Forcing Perturbations Against Unknown Detectors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05369)
*Chia-Hsiang Lin,Si-Sheng Young,Jon Atli Benediktsson*

Main category: eess.IV

TL;DR: 本文首次提出抗高光谱异常检测（AHAD）技术，通过设计能量高效的隐身扰动信号，使地面关键目标在未知探测器位置信息的情况下，同时规避几乎所有主流数据驱动和模型驱动的HAD方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 高光谱异常检测技术使地面设施/目标完全暴露，现有研究缺乏有效的对抗手段，且传统对抗攻击方法不适用于此场景，亟需一种全新的反检测理论和技术。

Method: 提出ARAB正则化（将真实异常融入背景）和伪异常欺骗机制，通过Lipschitz强制扰动在特征空间平坦化拓扑增强的异常/背景结构；针对不完美CSI，采用矩阵移位失配建模不确定性，生成鲁棒扰动。

Result: 在多个真实数据集上验证了算法的有效性和鲁棒性，单个AHAD扰动信号能同时规避几乎所有基准检测器，并提出了新的量化评估指标ArmCBA。

Conclusion: 该研究填补了高光谱反异常检测领域的空白，为遥感对抗提供了全新的理论框架和实用工具。

Abstract: Hyperspectral imagery represents the best contemporary technology to remotely detect anomalous objects. Nevertheless, hyperspectral anomaly detection (HAD) technique makes ground facilities/situations completely exposed. For the first time, we develop the first anti-HAD (AHAD) technique rendering the key objects undetected, without perfect coordinate/position state information (CSI) of the detectors (e.g., reconnaissance aircraft). Our AHAD algorithm is generally applicable to defend against almost all the existing benchmark data-driven and model-driven HAD methods. AHAD is fundamentally different from conventional adversarial attacks, so novel theory is needed. We customize novel regularizers for assimilating real anomalies into the backgrounds (ARAB) and fooling the detectors with pseudo-anomalies, thereby optimizing an energy-efficient stealthy perturbation signal for AHAD. The ARAB regularization is mathematically interpretable as flattening the topology-enhanced anomaly/background structures in the feature space, hence termed Lipschitz-forcing perturbations. Considering the imperfect CSI, we further develop a robust AHAD criterion, where the uncertainty is mathematically described as matrix-shifting misalignment for statistically generating the robust perturbation. Comprehensive experiments demonstrate the effectiveness and robustness of our AHAD algorithm across diverse real-world datasets. Remarkably, our algorithm generates a single AHAD perturbation signal that can simultaneously evade almost all benchmark detectors, greatly enhancing its practicality, given that the reconnaissance detector type is usually unknown. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first formal AHAD study. As a side contribution, we propose a new quantitative performance index, ArmCBA, to evaluate the robustness of an HAD method against our AHAD signal.

</details>
